About me

I've spent the better part of two decades living and working at the intersection of banking infrastructure and human experience — the place where payment flows, regulatory complexity, and real people's needs all collide.

After studying in Heidelberg, I sharpened my analytical edge at PwC and built deep operational know-how at UBS in Switzerland. In between, I founded and ran my own venture — SDS, Schlosser Data Services — until it was acquired in 2015.

Today I lead the Customer Success Banks team at Omikron Systemhaus in Cologne, helping financial institutions navigate the fast-shifting world of corporate e-banking. Whether that means guiding a bank through the rollout of Instant Payments, implementing Verification of Payee, or rethinking how multicash can serve the next generation of treasury teams — I'm the person who sits between the technology and the humans who depend on it.

Alongside that, I have lectured at IU International University of Applied Sciences, teaching Business English and Change Management. There is something genuinely rewarding about watching a student connect language and leadership concepts to their working life for the first time.

And when I step away from all of that — I bake, I write, I surf when geography allows, and I'm a husband and father doing his best in Cologne.

On stage

The European payments landscape is transforming fast — and the gap between what's technically possible, what regulators require, and what banks actually understand is still enormous. That gap is where I speak.

My talks are built on years of front-line experience: implementing Instant Payments at scale, navigating the VOP rollout with real banking clients, and watching the ISO 20022 migration unfold from the inside. No theory for its own sake — just clear, honest insight from the coalface.

  • SEPA Instant Payments — from regulation to reality
  • Verification of Payee: what banks get wrong
  • ISO 20022 migration — practical lessons learned
  • The future of corporate e-banking
  • Customer success in complex banking software environments
  • Digital transformation in treasury management
