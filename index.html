Jahn Schlosser - Author

The author lived with his wife and daughter in Barcelona for many years. Surfing the city beaches and climbing the surrounding mountains where part of his everyday routine. He fell in love with the city about 15 years ago during a casual weekend visit. He returned every year and never dropped the idea of once changing his primary residence to Spain.
